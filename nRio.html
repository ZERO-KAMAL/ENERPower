Natural Resources in Indonesia

Indonesia is a nation rich in natural resources and with increasing energy demand to fuel the contry's continued economic growth. Indonesia is one of the world's largest producers and exports of coal, having produced 616 million tons of coal in 2019 and exported about 455 million tons, of whuch thermeal accounted for 428.8 million tons in 2019
Since 2005, it overtook Australia and became leading exporter in terms of thermal coal. Three largest region of Indonesia coal resources are

  1. South Sumatia
  2. South Kalimanton
  3. East Kalimanton
